• Aim for 7-9 hours of sleep each night to ensure that your body and mind are well-rested and functioning at their best.

    • Create a relaxing bedtime routine, such as reading or taking a warm bath.
    • Avoid screens (e.g., phones, laptops, TVs) for at least an hour before bedtime. The blue light emitted by screens can interfere with sleep.
